# Cohort funnel — CRO & product additions (Fable, 2026-07-17)

Net-new suggestions beyond the flow-vision v2, ordered by funnel position. Every item grounded in the funnel audit numbers (81% blended abandon today; form completion 91% at 7 fields / 26% at 25 / 14% at 44; 459 stalls at one qualifying question; 69% of abandoners left phone+email; 0/199 payments carried an app id). **Each is a RAHUL DECISION — none is assumed locked until he confirms.**

## The three structural bets (highest measured leverage)
1. **Invert the funnel: pay first, qualify after.** Pre-payment Tally form shrinks to ~5 fields (name, phone, email, craft, availability) → ₹400 fee framed as *reserving a review slot* → essay/quiz/portfolio move AFTER payment. Attacks the biggest measured loss (form-length abandonment) and fixes essay rage-typing (only paid, serious people write it). Mostly Tally reordering, minimal engineering.
2. **Kill the Calendly gap: slots on the ₹400 success screen.** Three soonest interview slots as one-tap buttons on the payment-success page. "Fee paid, interview not scheduled" is born in the hour between paying and scheduling — close it to zero.
3. **Certificates with honors tiers (Distinction / Merit / Completion)** computed from attendance + submissions, shown as a live "academic standing" line in the room from week 1. Turns the most shareable artifact into a 12-week attendance engine. Recordings reframed as *recovery* (protects standing), not a substitute for attending.

## Application & re-entry
4. **The reminder link IS the login** — every nudge carries a magic link that authenticates and lands on the exact unfinished field. Two taps total.
5. **"₹400, credited toward tuition if accepted."** Reframes fee → deposit. (Decision: actually credit it — rounding error vs conversion lift at these fee sizes.)
6. **Honest scarcity always** — surface REAL batch caps + review-batch close ("14 seats remain · 212 applications this week"). Never fake urgency.

## Interview → acceptance
7. **Prep pack as a selectivity device** — a 2-min "what strong applicants do" note post-booking. Raises show-rate, answer quality, and felt selectivity.
8. **Lapsed ≠ lost** — if the 24h confirmation window expires, seat releases but acceptance stays valid for the next batch (said upfront). Removes deadline resentment; builds pre-sold pipeline.
9. **Confirmation-fee EMI / UPI-autopay at the euphoria moment** — offer the remainder as structured autopay seconds after the happiest screen.

## Pre-cohort & in-cohort
10. **Assignment Zero** — one 30-min craft-relevant micro-assignment submitted in the room before day one. Breaks WhatsApp-limbo, builds the room habit at peak excitement, and is the earliest at-risk signal (whoever skips it skips week 3).
11. **The cohort map** — confirmed students as dots on an India map with craft tags. Belonging + social proof, zero "you're student #3" risk.
12. **The dailies wall** — weekly submissions visible to the cohort; mentor highlights three each feedback session. Cheapest quality-and-attendance pressure; seeds in-room community with real content.

## Loops that feed the top of funnel
13. **Every artifact carries a door** — admission film, acceptance card, certificate each end with a tracked "Applications open · [cohort] · [batch]" line. Status becomes an acquisition channel; measure share→application.
14. **The payment ledger** — one screen: every rupee paid (₹400 → ₹8k → remainder), what each unlocked, what remains. Trust artifact + dispute killer + makes flexible per-SKU fees legible.
15. **Name the first three A/B tests now** — analytics exist since phase 3. Commit to testing: (a) fee-position inversion, (b) success-page slot embed, (c) essay before/after payment.

**Fable's ranking if only three ship: #1, #2, #3** — they attack the three biggest measured losses and none waits on the app build (#1/#2 are largely Tally + Razorpay config).
